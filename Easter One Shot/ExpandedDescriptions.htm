The Hollow Warren

Expanded descriptions of the Cloverwick Dungeon
Room Reference
🌿 Area 1 — The Mouth

Hawthorn-framed entrance, packed smooth walls threaded with roots. Clover grows pink instead of white. Faint sweetness in the air.

  • Encounter: Giant Warren Hare (HD1, AC12, d4 bite). Frightened, not hostile. DC 10 WIS to read it. Calming it = future ally.
  • Egg 1: Pink clover patch DC 6 WIS
  • Egg 2: Hollow of a glowing root, high on wall DC 8 STR/DEX
  • Egg 3: Half-buried under muddy boot prints DC 8 WIS
🔀 Area 2 — The Burrow Cross

Junction chamber. Left: warm lavender glow (Corruption). Right: dark, smells of chocolate. Scratch-mark art filled with pastel clay. Child's basket at center.

  • No combat — atmosphere + choice. Scratching sounds from both paths.
  • Egg 1: Inside the basket, mixed with straw — free find
  • Eggs 2: Behind disturbed clay in the scratch-art DC 10 WIS
  • Eggs 3–4: Cache under the basket DC 8 WIS
🛒 Area 2b — The Warren Market

30ft bubble chamber. Three Merchant Hares in cloth aprons run stone stalls lit by luminous mushrooms. Swept floor, colorful root-banners.

  • Stall 1 → Pastel Mist Vial. Wants something shiny.
  • Stall 2 → Corrupted Carrot. Wants something sweet.
  • Stall 3 → Warren map on bark. Wants something soft.
  • Steal/attack = shriek → 1d4 Pastel Rabbits arrive in 1 round.
  • Eggs 1–3: On Stall 1 display — must be traded for
  • Egg 4: In cloth pouch on root-banner overhead DC 8 WIS
  • Egg 5: Behind luminous mushrooms DC 6 WIS
  • Egg 6: Under swept floor, scrape of dirt DC 10 WIS
🐣 Area 3 — The Nursery

Rose-pink walls with slowly rotating swirl patterns. Dozens of glowing pastel eggs in alcoves. Thick dry grass floor. Two children asleep in center nest — unharmed, Sugared Slumber.

  • Encounter: 2× Pastel-Touched Rabbits. On hit: roll Chromatic Sneeze (d4).
  • Loot: Candy Potion in one alcove egg (heals 1d4 HP).
  • Eggs 1–2: Buried in nest grass around children DC 6 WIS
  • Egg 3: Inside a cracked alcove egg DC 8 WIS
  • Egg 4: Flush with pink wall, camouflaged DC 12 WIS
  • Eggs 5–6: Cache beneath raised grass DC 8 WIS
  • Egg 7: High alcove ledge — visible DC 6, climb DC 8 DEX
🖼️ Area 3b — The Painted Hall

Long narrow tunnel, smoothed walls covered in illuminated-manuscript murals. Peach lantern hangs at midpoint. Leather satchel on root peg. Final mural reads: "It just wants a friend."

  • DC 8 INT — Cult sealed idol because it eroded free will with joy. Hints at re-sealing.
  • DC 10 WIS — Child's paint-stained fingerprints at child-height on final mural.
  • Entering: DC 8 WIS or spend first round stunned by the colors.
  • Satchel: Cultist Notes (advantage on re-sealing check) + 8cp
  • Egg 1: Inside the leather satchel — auto-find
  • Egg 2: Painted into the mural wall — real depth DC 10 WIS
  • Egg 3: Atop the peach lantern — loud if disturbed DC 8 WIS
  • Egg 4: Floor crack near final mural DC 8 WIS
  • Egg 5: Behind painted false door in mural DC 12 WIS/INT
🍬 Area 4 — The Candy Vein

Amber-gold crystallized sugar seam from floor to ceiling. Crystal-furred walls scatter rainbow light. Floor is slick and glittering. Child's scarf caught on a jagged outcropping.

  • Encounter: Sugar Sprite (HD1, AC13, d4 shard throw, flies). Territorial. Can trade: one colored egg → Crystal Shard (difficult terrain item).
  • Hazard: Slick floor — move quickly = DC 8 DEX or prone.
  • Egg 1: Caught with child's scarf — auto-find
  • Eggs 2–3: Embedded in the vein, visible as shapes DC 8 WIS + 1 round to chip free
  • Egg 4: Frozen flat in crystal floor DC 10 WIS
  • Egg 5: Hollow behind the vein — Sprite retrieves it if combat starts DC 8 WIS

Encounters · Boss · Corruption · Loot · Endings
Stat Blocks

Giant Warren Hare

HD 1 (5 HP) AC 12 MV Fast (40ft) ATK Bite d4

Frightened: Not hostile. DC 10 WIS to read its fear. Calming it makes it a future ally — it reappears at Area 5 entrance if befriended.

Pastel-Touched Rabbit

HD 1 (4 HP) AC 11 MV Normal ATK Bite d4

Chromatic Sneeze (on hit, d4): 1 = target glows, disadvantage on stealth; 2 = smells of flowers, animals skip their attack this turn; 3 = hair turns random color, no effect; 4 = euphoric, DC 10 WIS or spend action laughing.

Merchant Hare (×3)

HD 1 (4 HP) AC 10 MV Fast ATK Flee (non-combatant)

Non-hostile. Communicate via nudging and placing items. Threatened → shriek → 1d4 Pastel Rabbits arrive in 1 round. Hares flee.

Sugar Sprite

HD 1 (5 HP) AC 13 MV Fly 30ft ATK Shard throw d4

Crystallize: On failed DC 10 DEX, target's feet are stuck for 1 round.

Trade: Offer a colored egg → receives Crystal Shard (throw to create difficult terrain, 10ft radius, 2 rounds).

Final Boss

☠ The Ovum Eternal

HD 2 HP 10 (14 if Lv3 party) AC 14 (stone) MV None (immobile)
ATK Pastel Bolt (ranged, d6)

Chromatic Aura (passive): Once per player turn, roll d6 on Corruption table. Effect applies to everyone in the room.

Summon Touched (1/combat): Calls 1 Pastel Rabbit. Triggers only if players ignore the enthralled child for 2 full rounds.

Shell of the Eternal (1/combat, 2× if Lv3): +4 AC for 1 round.

Weak Point: A glowing crack pulses brighter when damaged — telegraphs it's beatable.

Re-seal (good ending): Press a colored egg to the idol and speak a lullaby/prayer. DC 10 CHA or WIS (advantage if Cultist Notes held). Idol goes dormant.

Destroy: Deal damage until 0 HP. Corruption fades immediately.

Endings & Rewards
✨ Best Ending — Re-Seal
  • All three children rescued and returned safely.
  • Corruption fades; warren returns to normal.
  • The befriended Warren Hare follows players out and nuzzles them.
  • Reward: 20gp village reward + feast + one Cloverwick Egg per player (reroll any single die once before next long rest).
⚔️ Standard Ending — Destroy
  • Corruption fades immediately. Warren hare grateful.
  • Reward: 20gp village reward + feast.
  • No Cloverwick Eggs — players don't get the bonus.
Quick DM Reminders
  • Light: Enforce Shadowdark's real-time torch rules — the warren is naturally dark outside corrupted zones.
  • Corruption zones: Areas 3, 3b, 4, and 5 count as "corrupted." Roll the table there if the mood calls for it, or save it for Area 5 only.
  • The children: Kids 1 & 2 are asleep in Area 3 (can be carried, slow movement). Kid 3 is enthralled in Area 5 — roleplay snaps them out (DC 10 CHA).
  • The Warren Hare ally: If befriended in Area 1, it reappears outside Area 5 and can be sent in as a distraction (draws 1 Pastel Rabbit's attention for 2 rounds).
  • Flex rooms: Warren Market (2b) and Painted Hall (3b) can be cut if running short on time.
  • On re-sealing: Let players get creative — a lullaby, a prayer, a spring poem, or a heartfelt plea all qualify. Reward good roleplay with auto-success.
